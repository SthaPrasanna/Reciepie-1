Delicious Chicken Katti Roll

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, thinly sliced or cut into small pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

For assembling the rolls:

  • 4-6 rotis or parathas (you can use store-bought or homemade)
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Lemon wedges
  • Mint chutney or any other sauce of your choice

Directions:

  1. Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies to the pan. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  4. Now, add the sliced chicken to the pan along with cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well to combine all the spices with the chicken.
  5. Cook the chicken mixture until it is fully cooked and tender. Stir occasionally to prevent burning. If needed, you can sprinkle a little water to prevent the chicken from drying out.
  6. Once the chicken filling is ready, remove it from heat and set it aside.
  7. Warm up the rotis or parathas on a skillet or tawa.
  8. To assemble the rolls, place a portion of the chicken filling on one side of the roti or paratha. Top it with some sliced onions, cucumbers, chopped coriander leaves, and a drizzle of mint chutney or sauce of your choice.
  9. Squeeze some lemon juice over the filling for an extra burst of flavor.
  10. Roll up the roti tightly, enclosing the filling, to form a roll or wrap.
  11. Serve the Chicken Kathi Rolls hot with extra mint chutney or sauce on the side.
